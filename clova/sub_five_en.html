RESEARCH VIDEO
English Korean

Deep learning and AI technologies have already made their way into our daily lives. They understand our language; recommend music; suggest and summarize news; tell us about a random flower we photographed; translate menus and signs in foreign languages; and enable us to read documents and publications in other languages with much greater ease. These technologies will continue to permeate further and deeper into our lives, transforming all aspects of our world. With the dawn of this new era, researchers at CLOVA AI Research conduct studies and develop these deep learning and AI technologies looking to truly understand what this exciting new future can bring.
